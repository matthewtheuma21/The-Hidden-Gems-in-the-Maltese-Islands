St.Philips Garden
950m away from Tritoni Fountain (Valletta Entrance)

last updated: 19/04/2024

A very-well historic garden has recently been open again to the public after restoration. However, more works on the entrance and of the Sally Port are still to be done as to make the area more accessible. The fountain that can be found in the center of the garden, which was built to celebrate the construction of the Wignacourt aqueduct. It was originally found in Saint George’s square but was then moved to the Saint Philip Garden grounds.

This place would is a perfect destination to have a calm and peaceful rest both for the city’s guests and locals.

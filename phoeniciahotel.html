Floriana
200m away from the city gate of Valletta

last updated: 14/04/2024

This classical five star hotel surrounded by gorgeous landscaped gardens is both a big landmark and a luxury retreat. It blends history, culture and luxury together as a hotel. In addition, the hotel is just outside the vibrant capital of Valletta on the edge of the bastions, which make it an excellent location. The construction of the Phoenicia was interrupted by the outbreak of World War II, when the building was requisitioned by the British military to be used by Royal Air Force personnel. It was then highly targeted by an air raid on April 27th, 1942; estimated over 100 bombs were dropped on the grounds. In 1948, the hotel was finally opened, featuring a beautiful spa, an amazing outdoor pool (with a glass panel on the edge, opposite the beautiful view of Malta from the top), retail offerings on designer sunglasses to high-class perfumes and more.

Its interior includes pretty authentic tiled floors, uplifted colours of the mediterranean (blue and white) and textures in old-world elegance mixed with modern style. It has been refurbished through the years, extending another floor.

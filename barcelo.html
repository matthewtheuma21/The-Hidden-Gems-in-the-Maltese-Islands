Barcelo Fortina Hotel, Sliema
400m away from the shopping center Tigne Point

last updated: 20/04/2024

A stunning five-star hotel on the seafront promenade in Sliema, that has recently been refurbished to unique architecture. Its location is surrounded by a wide range of shops and other restaurants and tourist attractions. The hotel stands out for its panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and fortified city of Valletta. They offer a variety of services and amenities with its spa and wellness centre, indoor cocktail bar, outdoor pool with a beach club (were you can relax and enjoy the front view there), one heated indoor pool, a big modern gym that is for surely design for anyone, including a lot of people and some more.

In summer, they invite a DJ to entertain the people enjoying their time at the pool, giving them a top experience.

All of the rooms have a terrace, with perfect luxurious and modernsetting inside that make a comfortable stay.

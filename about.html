Currently a student in MCAST Mosta, taking Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design Lvl 5. I am really into photography, creating art on such digital softwares and making forms of advertisment.

I always have been dedicated to designing my work in a vibrant layout, from writing my school notes to anything that we as graphic designers create. However, I love making use of monochromatic/black + white palettes with a sense of aesthetic in my work.

Maybe my least favourite medium, would be working on animation, as its not really my go-to to work with, because I am more into creating printed visuals and design.

I also have a passion for travelling around the world, exploring new stuff and achieving goals to every step of my life.

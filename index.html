The Little hamlet in Fawwara, Siġġiewi

In a little charming hamlet called Fawwara, lies two chapels around a scenic countryside (with lush greenery rocky cliffs, scenic valleys) making it a haven for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. It is also a great spot for hiking, as it brings solitude and relaxation, away from the hustle and bustle of urban life. The two rural chapels are situated near the cliffs of Fawwara, dating back to the medieval period. The hamlet itself is part of Malta's cultural and religious heritage, offering visitors a glimpse into the island's history and legends that lie there.

Barcelo Fortina Hotel, Sliema

Escape to paradise at the Barceló Fortina Spa Resort in vibrant Sliema, Malta! Indulge in a world of luxury where every moment is designed to delight your senses. The hotel offers a range of room options, from standard digs to swanky suites, so you can pick what suits your vibe and budget. One of the big draws is its spa and wellness facilities. They've got everything from saunas and Jacuzzis to massage treatments and beauty services. It's literally like a relaxation paradise! Foodies won't be disappointed either. The hotel boasts a variety of dining options, including Mediterranean cuisine and international fare. And let's not forget about the bars – perfect for unwinding with a cocktail after a long day of exploring Malta.

Ulysses Lodge, Xagħra

A mysterious allure that captivates adventurers and urban explorers alike. Situated on the picturesque island of Gozo, part of the Maltese archipelago, this abandoned lodge holds secrets waiting to be uncovered. Once was a bustling hub of activity, the Ulysses Lodge now stands frozen in time, its weathered facade hinting at a storied past. From the overgrown gardens to the crumbling walls, every corner of the lodge tells a tale of bygone days. Despite its dilapidated state, the lodge exudes a haunting beauty that attracts photographers, historians, and curious travelers seeking to unravel its history. It's a place where imagination runs wild, conjuring visions of grandeur and mystery.

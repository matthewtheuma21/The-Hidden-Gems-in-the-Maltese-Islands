Hilton Hotel, Portomaso (St.Julians)
400m away from Paceville (night life area)

last updated: 14/04/2024

A luxurious five star hotel with a beautiful marina behind it; the Hilton chain that upholds high standards. The hotel is situated in the city of St. Julians next to the Portomaso Casino, and the great nightlife of Malta. They have a spacious outdoor pool and its unique indoor pool, hydrotheraphy, steam and sauna facilities, tennis court, fitness center, yoga sessions and their own club.

The hotel provide mediterranean flavours and a taste of Thailand in the ‘Blue Elephant’ Restaurant. The other restaurants are so beautiful that it really makes the hotel stand out. They’re very spacious, iconic and elegant. The Quaterdeck Bar has a nautical theme and dark leather armchairs, were guests get to relax on Jazz night.

Kempinski Hotel, San Lawrenz (Gozo)


2km away from The Basilica of the National Shrine of the Blessed Virgin of Ta' Pinu

last updated: 20/04/2024

Another Hotel Chain of Kempinski from Berlin, which is a five star hotel located in Gozo, providing high levels of luxury and style, excellent in privacy. It is in fact situated in a blissful countryside on the outskirts of San Lawrenz, but close to many touristic attractions that Gozo offers. They feature elegant rooms, suites and even apartments that make your stay comfortable. In addition, the hotel has unique restaurants and bars with a blend of international flavours, along their beautiful garden. You can also make use of their tennis court if you like playing such sport. Their parking may be limited depending on the amount of cars/people staying there, but it really isn't a common problem.

A quick fact They make use of their ‘Kempinski White Glove Service’, which they believe its a huge symbol of luxurious service, following the health and safety recommendations, along with many other well-being performances.

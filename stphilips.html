St. Philips Hospital - St.Venera
1.4km away from Msida Skate Park

last updated: 23/04/2024

100-bedded capacity hospital located in Santa Venera was closed in 2012 after running into financial problems. Most of its entrances are blocked up with rubble wall, however there are some few other entrances to enter, and you can still explore the whole building and its rooms. Some rooms are left with burnt chemicals spread on the floor, many documents from past patients with their information, broken televisions and other prdocucts dating back to when they were used at that time (simply a coca cola bottle with the expire date being on 2010). Some rooms at the underground level are completly dark, thats where one can find big mechanisms that were being used.

So please do be aware when visiting this place, to be careful of the surroundings and bring a flashlight with you. Also don't go alone. It is believed that some or two live there, as you may find rooms with personal belongings folded nicely until now, so its very clear that this is true.

Mellieħa
3.8km away from Coral Lagoon, Mellieħa

last updated: 13/04/2024

A four star hotel, situated along Malta’s northern coast, making it close to our beautiful beaches in the North (Ramla Bay, Armier Bay, Għadira Bay, Paradise Bay, and more) and other places to visit around the locality. The hotel offers excellent facilities for both leisure and business stays. Guests can enjoy the private beach or opt for the three pools outside/the indoor pool. The resort also provides various dining experiences (restaurants, bars and lounges).

Its interior is also spectacular, one of a kind in Malta. The corridors feature a beautiful indoor garden in an organic, dynamic and curved formation that give beautiful views. Its latest ongoing project makes it stand out more in the following years on its ongoing extension as a hotel.

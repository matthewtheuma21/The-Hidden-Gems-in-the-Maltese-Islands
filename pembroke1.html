Pembroke
220m away from the clock tower of Pembroke

last updated: 19/04/2024

This sculpture was build behind the purpose of having all our museums and attractions closed during the covid pandemic, being the concept of community outreach and art in public spaces (which has now gained a new dimension). The sculpture symbolises the architecture of the British surrounded around Pembroke, specifically from the clock tower.

Originally, the completion of the sculpture was meant to be complemented by a live performance piece by Nicole Bugeja, however COVID-19 requirements led to a change in plan, with the project instead being filmed at different times of the day, in different seasons, in different weather, light, temperature and noise conditions.

The sculpture lies within a pretty picnic area facing the Pembroke clock tower, surrounded by trees and open spaces.

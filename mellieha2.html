Paradise Bay - Mellieħa
3.6km away from The Ramla Bay Resort in Mellieħa

last updated: 29/04/2024

This particular bay is one of the most recommendable places to visit for snorkling lovers. The whole bay crosses a range of depth, still with a sea-bed of sand, which makes of the sea look bright and beautiful. The area around the shoreline is all rocks forming small parts (surrounded with other big rocks) where you can sit on the rocks and paddle your feet in the sea. It’s a nice experience to look around this place, both to relax and to enjoy snorkeling in the crystal clear sea.

On the left side of the area, there is a nice restaurasnt where you can enjoy the view of the bay itself, and you can also rent sunbeds from here. However the section is divided into classes, the sunbeds that are on their own built platforms have their own privacy were no one will be in front of you; however it is important to note that if you rent a sunbed on the sand, many tourists will come block your view and make their own spot. In the afternoons, the place starts to get busy.
